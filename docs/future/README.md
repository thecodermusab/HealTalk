# Future Plans — HealTalk

Ideas and features planned for future versions of HealTalk.

## Potential Features

### Payments
- Integrate Stripe for real payment processing
- Patient pays at booking, psychologist receives payout
- Refund handling for cancelled appointments
- Invoice generation

### Group Sessions
- Allow psychologists to host group therapy sessions
- Patients can join as a group video call (up to 8 people)
- Separate booking flow for group sessions

### Mobile App
- React Native or Expo app for iOS and Android
- Push notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging
- Offline mood logging

### AI Enhancements
- Smarter chatbot with conversation memory (store chat history per user)
- AI-generated session summaries for psychologists
- Mood trend analysis with AI insights

### Telehealth Compliance
- Full HIPAA compliance audit
- End-to-end encrypted messaging
- Data residency options (US, EU)

### Hospital Integrations
- Partner hospitals can refer patients directly to psychologists on the platform
- Hospital staff accounts with special permissions
- Integrated scheduling with hospital systems

### Analytics
- Detailed patient progress reports for psychologists
- Platform-wide mental health trend reports (anonymized)
- Psychologist performance dashboards

### Reviews and Ratings
- Patients can review psychologists after a completed session
- Verified reviews only (must have completed at least one session)
- Psychologist can respond to reviews

### Community / Resources
- Public mental health blog with psychologist-authored posts
- Podcast library
- Self-help guides and exercises

### Subscription Plans
- Patients can subscribe for a set number of sessions per month
- Discounted rates for subscribers
- Family plans (cover multiple family members)
